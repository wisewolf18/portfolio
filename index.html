Accident Detection using android

"The 'Accident Detection using Android' project represents a significant step towards enhancing road safety through cutting-edge technology. In this innovative undertaking, we developed a robust Android-based system that utilizes sensors, GPS, and real-time data processing to swiftly detect and respond to vehicular accidents. By leveraging the power of smartphones and the capabilities of modern Android applications, our system is designed to automatically detect unusual changes in vehicle motion, sudden deceleration, or collision-like events. When such an incident is identified, the app sends immediate alerts to predefined emergency contacts and relevant authorities, providing critical location information. This project not only showcases our proficiency in mobile app development but also underscores our commitment to leveraging technology for the greater good, potentially saving lives and reducing emergency response times in the event of accidents."
